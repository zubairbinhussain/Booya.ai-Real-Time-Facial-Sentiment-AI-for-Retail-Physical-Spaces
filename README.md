# Booya.ai — Real-Time Facial Sentiment AI for Retail & Physical Spaces

> Computer vision platform analyzing human emotion at scale across live physical environments.

🔗 [booya.ai](https://booya.ai)

## Overview

Booya.ai is a real-time facial sentiment intelligence platform designed for physical environments. The platform analyzes human emotion at scale using computer vision and AI to help businesses in retail, automotive, entertainment, and media understand how customers genuinely feel during live experiences — enabling clients to optimize in-person engagement, improve satisfaction, and make real-time decisions backed by live sentiment data rather than post-experience surveys.

## Problem

Businesses operating in physical environments had no way to measure real customer emotional responses during live experiences. Traditional feedback methods like surveys and reviews captured sentiment hours or days after an experience, making it impossible to respond or adapt in the moment. Retail stores, car showrooms, entertainment venues, and media companies were making engagement and layout decisions based on incomplete, delayed, and often inaccurate customer feedback, with no scalable system to capture authentic emotional reactions across hundreds of simultaneous customer interactions.

## Solution

Built a real-time facial sentiment intelligence system using computer vision and deep learning to detect, classify, and analyze human emotional responses across physical environments at scale. The platform processes live video feeds in real time, identifying sentiment signals across multiple customers simultaneously without disrupting the environment. Emotion data is aggregated into actionable intelligence dashboards that give business teams live visibility into customer sentiment, engagement levels, and satisfaction signals during active experiences — operating continuously across retail floors, showrooms, entertainment venues, and media screenings.

## Responsibilities

- Built computer vision models for real-time facial expression detection and emotion classification
- Developed the AI sentiment analysis pipeline for processing live video data at scale
- Integrated real-time data processing with live business intelligence dashboards
- Optimized model performance for accurate emotion detection across diverse physical environments
